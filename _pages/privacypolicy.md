# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 11 January 2026_

One Clear Day (“the app”, “we”, “us”) is designed to be private by default.  
This Privacy Policy explains what data the app uses, how it is stored, and the choices you have.

If you have any questions about this policy, you can contact us at:  
**info@ppsdigitalmarketing.com**

---

## Overview

- We do not require accounts or email addresses  
- We do not collect personal data on our servers  
- All app data is stored locally on your device  
- You can delete your data at any time from within the app  
- We do not sell or share user data  

---

## Data We Store

The app stores the following information locally on your device in order to function:

- Daily reflections and review responses  
- Weekly review summaries (if used)  
- Selected values and identities  
- Completed actions  
- App preferences and settings  

This data exists solely to support the core features of the app.

---

## Where Your Data Is Stored

All data is stored locally on your device.

- We do not operate user accounts  
- We do not store data on external servers  
- We do not associate data with personal identifiers  

The app works without creating an account or providing personal information.

---

## Data Retention

By default, the free version of the app retains a limited window of recent data.  
Premium users may retain a longer history.

Regardless of tier, all stored data remains on your device and is never transmitted to us.

---

## Data Deletion

You can delete all stored app data at any time by using the **“Delete my data & reset app”** option in the app’s Settings.

This action:
- Permanently deletes all locally stored data
- Resets the app to a first-launch state
- Does not require contacting support

Once deleted, data cannot be recovered.

---

## Backup and Export

The app may allow you to manually export or import your data for personal backup purposes.

- Any exported files remain under your control
- We do not receive or store exported data
- Passphrases used for export are never stored or transmitted

---

## Analytics and Tracking

We do not use third-party analytics, advertising trackers, or behavioural profiling tools.

We do not track you across apps or websites.

---

## Third-Party Services

The app uses only essential platform services required to operate on iOS and Android.

We do not integrate with social networks, advertising platforms, or data brokers.

---

## Children’s Privacy

The app is not directed at children under the age of 13.  
We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

## Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to how data is handled, this Privacy Policy will be updated.

Any material changes will be reflected here before new features are enabled.

---

## Contact

If you have questions or concerns about privacy or data handling, you can contact us at:

**info@ppsdigitalmarketing.com**
